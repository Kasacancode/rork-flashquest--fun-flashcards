## Full Description (iOS & Android)

Turn your notes into a game.

FlashQuest is a study app that makes flashcards competitive. Create decks from scratch, scan your handwritten notes with AI, or paste any text to generate cards automatically. Then study them across four modes designed to build real retention, not just familiarity.

FOUR WAYS TO LEARN
• Study: Flip through cards at your own pace. Rate your recall and let smart scheduling handle the rest.
• Quest: Timed multiple-choice rounds that score speed and accuracy. Choose Learn mode or Test mode.
• Practice: Go head-to-head against an AI opponent that adapts to your level.
• Arena: Challenge a friend in real time. Create a room, share the code, and compete on the same questions.

AI DECK CREATION
Point your camera at handwritten notes, a textbook page, or a whiteboard and get a full flashcard deck in seconds. Or paste any text and let AI extract the key concepts. Review and edit every card before saving.

SMART REVIEW SCHEDULING
FlashQuest tracks how well you know each card and schedules reviews based on memory science. Cards you struggle with come back sooner. Cards you know well wait longer. No manual scheduling needed.

PROGRESSION THAT KEEPS YOU GOING
• 20 levels across 5 rank bands
• 36 achievements across 8 categories
• Daily streaks with gentle reminders
• Per-deck mastery tracking
• Weekly accuracy trends and activity heatmaps

BUILT FOR PRIVACY
All your data stays on your device. No account required. Optional analytics are off by default and fully transparent.

8 built-in starter decks included. Create unlimited custom decks. Free to use.
